It's Peanutbutter Jelly Time

A guide to making the perfect peanut butter and jelly!

Step 1: Ingredients

  • Knife
  • Peanutbutter

    (My personal favorite is JIF creamy)

  • Jelly

    (My personal favorite is strawberry)

  • 2 Slices of Bread

    (The best bread is challah)

  • Plate

Step 1

Step 2: The Spread

  1. Spread your peanutbutter edge to edge on one of the pieces of bread
  2. Clean the knife by wiping of any excess peanutbutter on your fingers and eating it
  3. Spread jelly edge to edge on the other peice of bread

    (If you are not going to eat the sandwhich immediately, before covering the other peice of bread with jelly first spread a thin layer of peanutbutter edge to edge)

Step 2

Step 3: The Close

  • Close the sandwhich by placing the jelly on the peanutbutter
